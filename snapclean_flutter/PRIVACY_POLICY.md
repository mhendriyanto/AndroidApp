# SnapClean Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

SnapClean helps users manage screenshots by importing selected images, applying
timers, saving screenshots to folders, and restoring recently deleted items.

## Information We Collect

SnapClean collects the information needed to provide the app:

- Account email and username
- User-selected screenshots
- Screenshot metadata such as names, folders, timer status, and timestamps
- App preferences such as theme, default timer, cleanup behavior, and reminder
  settings

SnapClean does not scan the user's full photo library. Screenshots enter the app
only when the user selects them through the device photo picker.

## How Information Is Used

Information is used to:

- Sign users into their account
- Sync saved screenshots and folders
- Restore account data after reinstall or device changes
- Run screenshot timers and cleanup workflows
- Support password reset and username recovery

## Storage

SnapClean uses Firebase Authentication for accounts, Cloud Firestore for
metadata and settings, and Firebase Storage for selected screenshot files.

Local copies may also be kept on the device so screenshots can load quickly in
the app.

## Deletion

Users can delete screenshots in the app. Deleted screenshots appear in Recently
Deleted where they can be restored. A future permanent deletion control should
remove the screenshot metadata and associated storage object.

Users who need account or data deletion should contact the app support contact
listed on the store page.

## Data Sharing

SnapClean does not sell user data. Data is stored only to operate the app's
account sync, screenshot archive, and cleanup features.

## Contact

For privacy questions, use the support contact listed on the app store page.
